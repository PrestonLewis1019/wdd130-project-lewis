Home Treatment Sensors and Pumps

Many Diabetics use this it is called a Glucose monitor. They are supposed to use it before every meal and multiple times throughout the day. The reason why is because it allows you to check your blood sugar and do what is needed to keep your sugar in range.

Treatment is not always easy because of insurance. But it will include Insulin therapy which requires daily insulin injections or an insulin pump to manage their blood glucose levels. And regular monitoring of blood sugar levels is crucial to determine insulin needs and adjust diet. Also, a healthy diet and regular physical activity are important components of managing blood sugar levels. And most important is a diabetes care team provides education and support for managing the condition long-term.
